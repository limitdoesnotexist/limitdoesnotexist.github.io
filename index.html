Our Purpose

Our project aims to help rural area schools in Taiwan by providing them with resources like 3D printers and teaching them skills like drafting. We combine math concepts with our lessons to further enhance their learning and connect them to their school curriculum.

Our Impact

- Rasied over $10,000 from local businesses for mateirals like 3D printers

- Taught lessons on drafting, math, and 3D-Printing

- Provided outlook on possible career fields and studying abroad

- Provided Lessons for over 50 children
